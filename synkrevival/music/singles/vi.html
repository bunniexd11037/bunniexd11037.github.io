THIS IS THE FIRST SONY RELEASE FROM THE ALBUM TRAVELLING WITHOUT MOVING AND HAD ITS FIRST RADIO AIRING IN THE UK ON 8 JULY 1996. RELEASED ON 19 AUGUST, IT ENTERED THE UK CHARTS AT NUMBER THREE MAKING IT AN ALL TIME JAMIROQUAI BEST, AND DROPPED TWO PLACES TO NUMBER FIVE THE FOLLOWING WEEK. HOWEVER, IN THE UK RADIO AIRPLAY CHART, THE SINGLE MADE IT ALL THE WAY TO THE NUMBER ONE POSITION.

IN THE UK, THE CIN OFFICIAL YEAR-END TOP 100 CHART FOR 1996 (01 JANUARY - 31 DECEMBER 1996) PLACED VIRTUAL INSANITY AT NUMBER 39 WITH SALES AT AROUND 310,000 (16 WEEKS IN CHART).

THE VIDEO FOR THIS SINGLE WAS NOMINATED FOR THE SELECT MTV AWARD AS PART OF THE 1996 MTV EUROPE MUSIC AWARDS. THIS IS THE ULTIMATE MTV VIEWERS CHOICE AWARD. SELECT IS MTV'S DAILY INTERACTIVE 'JUKEBOX' SHOW WHICH RECEIVES OVER 4,000 CALLS FROM VIEWERS SELECTING THEIR FAVOURITE MUSIC VIDEOS EVERY DAY. SADLY, VIRTUAL INSANITY DID NOT WIN THE AWARD. JAY, HOWEVER, DID MAKE AN APPEARANCE AT THE CEREMONY TO PRESENT THE MTV BEST GROUP AWARD TO OASIS.

AT THE 1996 MUSIC OF BLACK ORIGIN AWARDS, VIRTUAL INSANITY WAS NOMINATED FOR A PRIZE IN THE BEST MUSIC VIDEO CATEGORY.

VIRTUAL INSANITY WON THE "BEST POP VIDEO" AWARD AT THE 'MUSIC WEEK CREATIVE AND DESIGN AWARDS' WHICH WERE HELD ON 14 APRIL 1997. PRODUCED BY JONATHAN GLAZER, THE VIDEO ALSO WON AWARDS FOR BEST CINEMATOGRAPHY AND SPECIAL EFFECTS.

JAMIROQUAI ARE ABSOLUTELY HUGE IN JAPAN, AND THIS SHOWED IN THE ZIP HOT 100 SINGLES FOR 1996 WHERE VIRTUAL INSANITY WAS PLACED AT NUMBER FIVE IN THE CHART.
