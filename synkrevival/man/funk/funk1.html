"The Champ" (side a)/"Sound of the witch doctors" (side b) - THE MOHAWKS(PM 719) 1968 Pama Records

"The Champ" was more of a northern soul scene tune that later on crossed over to the Hip-Hop crowd. It was well ahead of its time in 1968. The Mohawks album is literally non-existent for it was recorded for the UK only, and the band only ever recorded one album. The beginning of this tune is a direct Hip-Hop break, used by KRS1 and many other rap groups.
