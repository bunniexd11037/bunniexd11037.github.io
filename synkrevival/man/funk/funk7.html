"I don't know what it is, but it sure is funky" - RIPPLE (GRC 1004) 1973 (DJ copies not for sale) General Record Corp'.

An underground funk tune released 1972 on the GRC label, Atlanta. It crossed over to Northern crowd in early 1970's, then later on in the 70's & 80's onto the soul scene. Played by DJ's like Mastermind, Rapatak etc. Then later on in the end of the 80's, at the height of the rare groove scene, it crossed over again to the rare groove crowd, where it was used as a hype tune to get crowds going.
