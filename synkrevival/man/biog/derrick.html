Drums

Born in 27/03/62, Derrick learnt to play drums by banging on his mothers chairs and on telephone books. He joined the band after the release of "Emergency on Planet Earth", and claims to have some very strong Jazz and funk musical influences, including Harvey Mason and Level 42!. A true family man he also sites his daughter as one of his major influences in his life. His best Jamiroquai memory is of when his arms had to be sprayed with Ralgex after his first gig with them because he was playing the drums so hard!!
