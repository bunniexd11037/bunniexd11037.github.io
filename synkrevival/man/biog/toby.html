Keyboards/co-writer

Born October 29th 1970. As a youngster he studied classical piano, and musical talent seems to run in his family. His dad has made two records with Dudley Moore!! Making his first record when he was 17, he then discovered house music. From there he got a record deal with the dance label Fffr, and started making music under the name "Kleptomaniacs". He met Jay Kay through a mutual friend in 1992, and as they say, the rest is history.
