  Didgeridoo

As the didgeridoo player, Wallis has known Jay Kay the longest. They go back as far as Jay's Ealing days, when they used to go skateboarding together. Jay asked him to play his didgeridoo in the introduction of 'When you gonna learn' and he's been in the band ever since. He's a self-taught didgeridoo player, and his first didgeridoo was a plastic tube!
