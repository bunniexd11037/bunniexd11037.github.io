JAMIROQUAI IS SUPERSONIC!!! Jay Kay and the boys put on a great show. Even the songs that I didnt like so much were good.

From the first song to the encore of Canned Heat Jamiroquai had the audience on their feet dancing and singing.

Only one slight complaint is that they didnt perform any songs from the first album. Which is cool. Jay said that they like to do the new stuff.

All the hits from Return of the Space Cowboy, Alright, Morning Glory, Butterfly were great to hear live.

Great lighting, audience participation, great music and a great venue(Thanks 9:30 Club!) and one of the hottest bands around added up to one of the hottest shows of the year

Shout out to all the D.C. Jamiroquai fans and a special hello to the Italian guy interning in D.C. who asked for directions to the show. Hope you enjoyed it as much as I did.
