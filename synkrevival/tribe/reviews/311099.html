WoW! Jamiroquai´s concert held in Helsinki was really the Event of the year! Although the day was Sunday, the weather was gray and rainy outside, and most people were about to have an early wakening the next day, the Hartwall Arena was filled with exited, energetic fans of the band waiting the concert to be a mind-blowing jam. And the band didn´t fail their expectations!
As it has been told, Jamiroquai has turned their funky style of music usually played only in clubs into a world-wide success. And after seeing their performance live, I don´t wonder how; the band was superb, their playing was smooth and still very lively. And Jay Kay did show his skills being a fantastic singer with a magnificent voice and excellent technical skills. Not to mention his dancing... Nice! This group can really do great music both in studio and live!
When Jay Kay came back on stage along their last song (played as encore) Deeper Underground and asked the crowd if they were ready to jump, I´m sure no one stood still.
