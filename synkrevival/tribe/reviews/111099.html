 22/10/00 21:42:20
 Score : 7/10
As a fan I was looking forward to the concert for quite some time, but I came away a little disappointed. The sound was in one word bad, everything was distorted a lot more than the last Jamiroquai concert in the same Ahoy hall in 1997. This was a pity, because the show, lights and stage all were better. There were 2 video walls on the left and right, which worked out nicely. You could see all Jays dancing, close-ups of Toby, Derrick, Nick and the others, all well done with groovy effects. I think that due to the hurry (10/10 Berlin, 11/10 Rotterdam, 12/10 Stuttgart) they did not take the time to work on the sound ...

I came in at 9 oclock due to traffic problems, and they were already playing (?!?, very early). The last notes of Cosmic girl sounded cool, Jay was dressed in a white suit + feathers which fitted him nicely. Then came Planet Home, which was very good, easily the best song of the evening, with an intermezzo of Latin percussion. For Supersonic Wallis came onto the stage with the didge. The didge was wicked, but the drums, bass and keys were blurred together. Travelling without moving was completely robbed of its magic by the very boring mid-section. Everybody started dancing at the beginning but in mid-part almost everybody stopped. I wonder why they did it? High times was OK. From the first two albums they only did Light Years. The Rolling Stones cover Miss you was fine, everybody humping around. The last song before the end was of course Canned Heat, which also had some strange breaks which took the dancing out of it. Then, after a few minutes, the encore Deeper Underground. This last song was completely destroyed by the distortion. I know you have to play it a little over the top, but well, this was too much. Most guys/girls around me didnt like it much either (I was at the back of the Arena-part). And so they stopped playing at 10:20, which I think is like a football game being stopped after 65 minutes. One or two encores more would be very thoughful, but they were very clearly on a tight schedule. Thx, bye and off they went. All in all, I saw that more people were a little disappointed, but they do not write reviews ....
