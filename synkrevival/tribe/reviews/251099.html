25/10/1999 - Hamburg
 Overall score
9/10
Oh how I was looking forward to see this unique fantastic brilliant concert of Jamiroquai - and I didnt regret anything (like one school-free day or the ticket for the train) and it seemed to me all the other fans felt the same. Its hard to find special words to describe the atmosphere and the great sound. Maybe its because Im a little fanatical, but I dont find anything missing there. I felt like flying above the crowd, kicking people with my special dance style and hoping itll never take an end.
But it did. Now one year passed by and all I can do is count the days till the next concert of Jamiroquai here in Northern Germany. Joy
