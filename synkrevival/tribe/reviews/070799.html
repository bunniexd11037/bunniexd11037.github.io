What can I say, Jam has done it again--made it simply impossible for me to get comfortable in my seat! I was dancing from when the lights went down to when they came back on again--truly a treat of a show as anyone else who was there will attest.

I personally feel that JKs performance was extra lively and that his spirits were (pardon the pun) extra high because of the beauteous, natural surroundings of the Greek theater. The open-air auditorium, in my opinion, provoked an enhanced feeling of community and shared good vibes between the band and the fans.

I waited to hear the precious Butterfly, a beautiful slow jam from the new cd, but alas, I sensed from the start that this concert was going to be nothing but a continuous flow of tight grooves.

Out of all of the performances, my personal favorites were Alright, Travelling Without Moving, Space Cowboy, Too Young To Die, Soul Education, High Times, and Planet Home.

The evenings standout performances were the throbbing Supersonic and a head-banging, knockout rendition of Deeper Underground.

A truly choice concert--even better than the last one that I went to in 96 at the Universal. Thanks, Jam, for a memorable good time!
