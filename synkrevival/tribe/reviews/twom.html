Although Travelling Without Moving contains the U.S. friendly hit Virtual Insanity, this cd is much, much more than that one song. As an American, I feel that its a shame that my country wasnt given a proper introduction to Jamiroquai, by way of Emergency On Planet Earth or Return of the Space Cowboy, as both are absolutely intoxicating bits of ear candy.

Nevertheless, bypass the friendly Virtual territory and groove to treasures such as the Africano-Latino vibe of Use the Force, the mesmerizing grooves of Alright and High Times, and my favorite, the title cut.

Also noteworthy is the hidden gem at the end, Do You Know Where Youre Coming From? Cmon, if you havent given the rest of this cd a chance then youre seriously missing out on some great music.
