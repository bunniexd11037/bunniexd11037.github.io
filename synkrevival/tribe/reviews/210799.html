The show that I saw in fall 95 in NYC was one of the most moving experiences in my short life. Everyone was there to chill and vibe with the music. Which brings us to last weeks show in New York, which I was very excited for. The energy and musicianship was as good as ever, but it seemed that this was an event to push the new album more than anything(only 1 song from EOPE & ROTSC!). And seemingly a short set (90 minutes)Perhaps it has a bit to do with becoming the band that sony would like them to be (read:VERY successful). Nothing wrong with that, but catalog sales count too. The guys did a great job of keeping the energy high and the crowd hyped, my only complaints were the lack of older material and slow stuff(expected) and length of the show. I would no doubt pay to see them again, but I long for the feeling I got at the show in 95. For me, thats the gold standard, but this show was certainly worth the silver.
