The scenario is a totally full ValbyHall in Denmark. A difficult place to make an excellent sound (I know)... eventhough - It was not far from being just that... so therefore, my best regards to the soundengineers = good job.

Being on tour through several months can be tough to a band. But Jamiroquai didnt show any sighs of exhaustion. Actually, after a few songs I felt kind of embarrassed the crowd didnt groove, moved and danced more... J.K. and band was trying to loosen us up. Trying to synkronize a thousand watches... In short terms - The concert was more party on a wednesday that even the most funky dane could ever wish for... And yet (as usual) the polite danes find themselves, participating in a funk-soul-pop-dance-jazz-show without sequel match in 1999, without even moving the right foot... Before the final song... (Deeper Underground)... On behalf of Denmark = Im sorry, Im sorry, Im sorry... The audience wasnt even half as good as the band !!!

For thoose of you, who havent seen Jamiroquai yet, please be ready to dance. Just move and forget your normal facade at school or work. If you feel the need to move/groove around to the music of jamiroquai, please dont cheat yourself (or the band) for the happy, free and timeless sensation.... DONT HOLD BACK...

The band makes the music -
YOU make the party.

So forget britpop. The only way you can dance to britpop, is to swagger around drunk.
