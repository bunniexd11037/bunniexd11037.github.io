 Score : 9/10
What could I possibly say about Jamiroquai’s concert here in Montreal? Perhaps that they played like kings, nearly killing us with their funk? Or maybe that they absolutely rocked the house? How about saying that it was the best concert I have ever attended? Whatever I say, it all boils down to the fact that every aspect of the band, from the bassist to the vocals, from the drummer to the pianist, each and every aspect was absolutely amazing.

Right from the start J got the crowd totally involved with his little snippets of ‘wisdom’ and once the little snowball that is Jamiroquai funk got rolling………

The songs were played brilliantly- talk about tight! I can’t do enough justice to this band’s playing Friday night, nor could I mention any particular member of the band as each ‘player’ did his part. To perfection.

Highlights of the night were “Deeper Underground” (talk about loud- I think my lungs and throat nearly collapsed!), the opener High Times and a cover of Rolling Stones hit. The crowd sang the “ooo-ooo-woo” refrain along with J and once the song ended kept going. After a while when we still hadn’t stopped the band started up again and off we went one more time. To quote J: “You just made my fucking year.”!

My only gripes would be the looooooooong wait before the band came on(although the DJ played some good tunes) and the shortness of the encore. It would have been nice if the t-shirts weren’t so bloody expensive aswell, but we all have to make some money. I guess.

Well worth the money- what a show. I WILL be at the next one. Without a doubt.
