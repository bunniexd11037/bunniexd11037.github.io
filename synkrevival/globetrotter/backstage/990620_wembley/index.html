IN JUNE 1999, JAMIROQUAI ENTERTAINED THE UK TO PROMOTE THE NUMBER ONE ALBUM SYNKRONIZED, ENDING THE TOUR AT WEMBLEY ARENA IN LONDON, ENGLAND ON SATURDAY 19TH AND SUNDAY 20TH.

WE WERE THERE ARMED WITH A CAMERA ON BOTH NIGHTS TO TAKE PHOTOS EXCLUSIVELY FOR THE SITE. FOR THOSE WHO WERE THERE THIS SHOULD BRING BACK MEMORIES, AND FOR THOSE WHO WEREN'T, JUST ENJOY THE PICTURES AND MAKE SURE YOU SEE JAMIROQUAI PLAY LIVE WHEN THEY NEXT VISIT YOUR TOWN.
