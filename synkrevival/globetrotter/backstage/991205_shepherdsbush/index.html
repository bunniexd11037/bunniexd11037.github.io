AFTER FINISHING A HUGELY SUCCESSFUL TOUR OF THE EUROPE, AMERICA, CANADA AND JAPAN DURING THE SUMMER AND AUTUMN OF 1999, JAMIROQUAI FINISHED THE YEAR WITH A ONE OFF CONCERT IN AID OF HOMELESS CHARITY SHELTER ON DECEMBER 5TH.

WITH TICKETS SOLD OUT IN ONLY TWO HOURS, AND A RETURN TO THE DAYS OF PERFORMING IN SMALLER VENUES, THIS WAS GOING TO BE A NIGHT TO REMEMBER.
